Real Humans

Connect is a place you can be your full self at work: obscure hobbies, silly costumes, and unique perspectives are celebrated here. The other thing we celebrate? Shipping excellent work. We hold ourselves to high standards and work hard to deliver amazing results.

The world needs real social

Humans are happiest when we spend quality time with our loved ones. Period. We think it's time that "social" apps get out of the way and let people do what we're designed to do. As app developers, this means saying no to tactics that hijack human attention, stick people in endless content loops, and deliver distraction. Connect is different: a simple, useful app to make your real-life experience come together.

We mean business

Diverse perspectives make winning products (and cultures). Our team hails from Brazil, Italy, India, Belarus, Nigeria, England, Venezuela and USA. We're proud that "team" is the #1 thing our employees love about working here. We're also pretty excited that Mr. Wiggles, our resident pug, has more Instagram followers than everyone else combined.

San Francisco for the win!

Our penthouse office is nice. Hold a meeting on our roof deck, whiteboard in a cozy collaboration nook, or cook dinner with a colleague in our full kitchen. Kombucha on tap? Yes. Array of costume hats for effective communication with teammates? Of course. We offer daily lunch, a massive stash of superfood snacks and beverages, and dinner if you're working late. We are located 1 block off Market Street and Powell BART, in the heart of SOMA, which makes commuting a snap.
