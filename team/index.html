Connect is a place you can be your full self at work: obscure hobbies, silly costumes, and unique perspectives are celebrated here. The other thing we celebrate? Shipping excellent work. We hold ourselves to the highest standards and work hard to deliver the best results.

The world needs real social

Our relationships, our health and our culture is fraying due to social tech that puts digital distraction first and humans second. We're on a mission to change that. We are domain experts in brain science, relational psychology, and calm tech. We spent the last 10 months doing deep design research with our customers to create technology that supports the way that humans actually socialize in real life. Our tech works *with* the human operating system, rather than hijacks it. We think people will like that.
